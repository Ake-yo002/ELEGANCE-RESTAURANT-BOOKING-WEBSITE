Our Story

Discover the passion behind Elegance

Culinary Excellence Since 2010

Founded by Chef Marco Bellini, Elegance has been redefining fine dining in the city for over a decade. What began as a small bistro with 10 tables has grown into one of the most celebrated restaurants in the region.

Our philosophy is simple: source the finest ingredients, treat them with respect, and present them beautifully. We work closely with local farmers and international suppliers to bring you seasonal dishes that tell a story.

The restaurant's interior, designed by award-winning architect Lisa Monteiro, creates an atmosphere of understated luxury where every detail has been considered for your comfort.
